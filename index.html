ECO: Environmentally Conscious Operation

Build Your Own City!

Opening Scene

“Congratulations! The federal government has recently acquired a new piece of land and you have been tasked with building a new town on it.

You have a budget of $12 billion to create the best town possible. While planning your town, you must make 6 important decisions that will dictate its future success and impact on the environment.

Make wise decisions to put your small town on the map and make it stand the test of time and climate change!”

Energy Source

“After clearing the land, you are now ready to begin construction!

Your town needs an energy source to keep it running! Select how you want to power your city.”

Water Source

“Your town doesn’t have a water source. How will your residents quench their thirst and perform their basic bodily functions?! Select how you would like to source your water.”

Food Source

“Now that we’ve sourced all this water, all we need is some food to keep your citizens from going hungry. Select how you would like to source your food.”

Roads

“Time to connect it all together! How are your residents going to travel from far and wide to your town without any roads? Select what kind of roads you would like to build.”

Residential Housing

“Now that you’ve set up the infrastructure of your town, your residents are ready to move in! But they don’t have a place to live. Select the style of the residential homes you’d like to build.”

Public Transport Infrastructure

“Your residents would like to be able to get around without a car! Got room in your budget? If you do, select what type of public transportation you want to implement in your city.”

Congratulations on building your city! The number displayed is how well your city scored, both environmentally and productively.

Scores can be positive or negative, but the higher your score is, the more successful and environmentally friendly your city is!
How do you think you did? How difficult was it to make some of these choices? What sacrifices did you make and do you regret prioritizing some decisions over others?

Do you think your residents are happy? By playing ECO, we hope you get the chance to reflect on the hard decisions you had to make and the things one must sacrifice to be environmentally friendly.

Although they may be the harder and more costly decision to make, they will ultimately keep us and our earth happy.
